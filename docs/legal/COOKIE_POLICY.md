## What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They let the website remember information about your visit, like your preferred language or login state.

We also describe **similar technologies** here (local storage, session storage, pixels, beacons) because they serve the same purposes from a regulatory standpoint.

## What cookies we use

### Strictly necessary (always on)

These cookies are required for the Site to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store personal identifiers beyond what's needed for the requested service.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| `session` | Maintains your browsing session | Browser session |
| `consent` | Records your cookie-preference choice | 12 months |
| `csrf` | Prevents cross-site request forgery on form submissions | Browser session |

### Analytics (opt-in)

We use PostHog to understand how visitors find and use the Site so we can improve it. Analytics cookies are only set after you give consent via the cookie banner.

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|------|----------|---------|----------|
| `ph_*` | PostHog | Session and event tracking | 12 months |

PostHog is configured for EU data residency. We do not pass cookie data to advertising networks.

### Marketing (currently not used)

We do not currently use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or third-party marketing. If this changes we will update this Policy and ask for your consent again.

## Your choices

You can manage cookies in three places:

1. **The cookie banner** when you first visit the Site — accept all, accept necessary only, or set per-category preferences
2. **Your browser settings** — most browsers let you block or delete cookies. See:
   - [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
   - [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
   - [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)
   - [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
3. **Withdraw consent** at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in our Site footer

Blocking strictly-necessary cookies may break parts of the Site (e.g. forms or login).
